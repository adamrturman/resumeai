**About the Role**

Shape the future of Web3 by building fast, high-quality frontend applications. As a key part of our team, you’ll design and develop core features to create a smooth and responsive user experience. You’ll work closely with product, design, and blockchain teams to bring ideas to life from start to finish.

**Core Responsibilities**

Architect, develop, and maintain high-performance, responsive web applications using modern frontend technologies

Collaborate closely with product, design, and blockchain teams to deliver exceptional user experiences

Build pixel-perfect UI components from Figma mockups and design assets

Debug and troubleshoot issues across the full web stack

**Qualifications**

Bachelor’s in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent experience

3+ years in frontend development, with 1+ years in Web3/DeFi

Expertise in JavaScript, TypeScript, React ecosystem

Solid understanding of web performance optimization and best practices

Experience with [Web3.js](http://web3.js/), [Ethers.js](http://ethers.js/), and an understanding of Web3 principles (wallets, smart contracts)

**Soft Skills:**

Basic English verbal and written communication skills are required

Strong collaboration abilities in a remote, cross-functional team

Ownership mindset - “you build it, you run it”

Problem-solving skills with attention to detail

Quick learner in a fast-paced environment
